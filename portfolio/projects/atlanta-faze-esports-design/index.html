atlanta faze esports design

The scope of this project was to design social media graphics for the professional Call of Duty players on the Atlanta Faze team. I also designed various other assets such as wallpapers for fans to download and social media graphics to use on match days. From announcing upcoming matches to celebrating victories, these graphics bridged the gap between Atlanta Faze and their fanbase.
