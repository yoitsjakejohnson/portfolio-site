Central Washington University Football Graphic

In the realm of sports marketing and recruitment, I had the honor of collaborating with Central Washington University Football. The objective was to create a dynamic and compelling social media graphic template to serve as a powerful tool for recruitment and outreach.

The project's outcome was the development of a captivating and brand-aligned social media graphic template for Central Washington University Football. This template serves as a powerful recruitment tool, enabling them to convey their passion for the game and attract promising athletes to join their team.
