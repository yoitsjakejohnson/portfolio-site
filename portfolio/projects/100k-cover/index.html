100K Cover Artwork

In the vibrant world of music and visual artistry, I had the privilege of collaborating with the music artist Kyson Slizz for his latest project “100K.” Kyson approached me with a unique vision - to create captivating and personalized cover artwork that would reflect the essence of the music while immersing the audience in a vivid cartoon world. Kyson provided photos of himself and his featured friend. It was essential to capture their personalities and the song's vibe in the cartoon-style artwork.

To add an extra layer of creativity, an animated version of the cover artwork was created. This animated artwork breathed life into the characters and elements, offering a dynamic and eye-catching preview of the music project.
