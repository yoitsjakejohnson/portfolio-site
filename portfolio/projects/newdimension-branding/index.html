new dimension brand identity

New Dimension Virtual Reality Arcade is an exciting startup eager to launch its journey into the immersive realm of virtual reality. This project was born out of the need to provide a comprehensive creative solution for a brand-new company looking to establish a strong visual presence.

To kickstart the project I conducted a very thorough research phase. This involved understanding the startup's aspirations and business objectives. Through a well-crafted design brief, I ensured that every aspect of the project would align with New Dimension's vision. Regular meetings with the startup allowed for the exchange of ideas, work-in-progress presentations, and feedback sessions. This process ensured that every design element would resonate with New Dimension's goals. To breathe life into the brand, I explored a range of concepts and design directions. These explorations pushed the boundaries of creativity, from refining the logo to developing a cohesive identity that would set New Dimension apart in a competitive industry.

The final result of this project was the successful creation of a comprehensive brand identity package for New Dimension Virtual Reality Arcade. This included a logo, color palette, typography selection, and suite of visual assets designed to capture the virtual reality experience. The final brand identity now speaks volumes about New Dimension's commitment to innovation, creativity, and immersive entertainment as it takes its first steps in the startup landscape.
